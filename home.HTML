What is NABH?

NABH stands for National Accreditation Board for Hospitals. The board is part of the Quality Council of India. Its primary function is to establish and operate an accreditation program for healthcare organisations across India.

The board benchmarks and assesses the healthcare institutions based on the quality of services they are providing to their patients. It is a fully autonomous body and operates with the support of the healthcare industry, government, and consumers.
